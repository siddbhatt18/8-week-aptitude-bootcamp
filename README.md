# 8-Week Aptitude Study Plan for Placement Success

## Overview: 80-20 Approach
This plan focuses on the **20% of concepts that appear in 80% of placement tests**: Number Systems, Percentages, Ratios, Time-Speed-Distance, Profit-Loss, Averages, Probability, Permutation-Combination, and Basic Logical Reasoning.

## **WEEK 1: Number Systems & Basic Arithmetic**

### Core Concepts (20% Focus)
1. **Divisibility Rules** (2-12)
2. **LCM & HCF** (shortcuts)
3. **Remainders** (Wilson's theorem basics, negative remainders)
4. **Unit digits & Last two digits**
5. **Prime numbers** (up to 100)

### Daily Schedule (2 hours/day)
- **Days 1-2**: Theory + Basic problems (30 mins theory, 90 mins practice)
- **Days 3-5**: Mixed difficulty problems
- **Days 6-7**: Timed practice + Review mistakes

### Problems (Progressive Difficulty)

#### Beginner (Days 1-2)
1. **Find LCM of 12, 18, 24**
   - *Reinforces*: Prime factorization method
   
2. **What is the remainder when 17 is divided by 5?**
   - *Reinforces*: Basic remainder concept

3. **Find unit digit of 3^47**
   - *Reinforces*: Cyclicity patterns

#### Intermediate (Days 3-5)
4. **Find HCF of 1095 and 1168 using division method in under 45 seconds**
   - *Reinforces*: Euclidean algorithm speed

5. **What is the remainder when 7^83 is divided by 6?**
   - *Reinforces*: Remainder theorem, cyclicity

6. **Find last two digits of 17^23**
   - *Reinforces*: Binomial for last two digits

7. **How many numbers between 1 and 100 are divisible by 2 or 3?**
   - *Reinforces*: Inclusion-exclusion principle

#### Advanced (Days 6-7)
8. **Find remainder when 2^2^77 is divided by 7**
   - *Reinforces*: Fermat's little theorem basics

9. **What is the smallest number that leaves remainder 3 when divided by 5, 7, and 9?**
   - *Reinforces*: LCM application

10. **If N = 2^3 Ã— 3^4 Ã— 5^2, how many factors does N have?**
    - *Reinforces*: Factor formula (a+1)(b+1)(c+1)

### Weekly Target
- Solve 50+ problems
- Accuracy: 70%+
- Speed: 1.5-2 mins/problem

## **Final Tips for Placement Success**

### During Preparation
1. **Use pen-paper**: Don't rely only on calculators
2. **Time yourself**: Always practice with a timer
3. **Skip strategically**: In mocks, skip if >2 mins spent
4. **Pattern recognition**: Notice common question types

### During Actual Test
1. **Read instructions**: Negative marking? Sectional cutoffs?
2. **Easy first**: Scan all questions, pick low-hanging fruits
3. **Accuracy > Attempts**: 20/25 better than 15/30
4. **Last 5 mins**: Guess only if no negative marking

### Psychological Preparation
- **Week 1-4**: Be patient, concepts take time
- **Week 5-6**: You'll feel comfortable with formulas
- **Week 7-8**: Confidence peaks, trust your preparation
